# AI Features in Stockpot

Stockpot uses AI to help you capture and create recipes faster. Here's what you can do:

## Import from Social

Share a recipe video from Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube directly to Stockpot. We'll extract the recipe from the post description.

## Import from URL

Paste any recipe link and we'll extract the full recipe for you. Works with most recipe websites and blogs.

## Generate with AI

Describe what you want to eat and get recipe ideas. You can even include your pantry items to get suggestions based on what you have on hand.

## Extract from Clippings

Saved some recipe text as a clipping? Convert it into a structured recipe with one tap. Also works for extracting shopping lists.

## Discover

Browse popular recipe websites from within the app and import recipes you find.

AI features require a Plus subscription.
